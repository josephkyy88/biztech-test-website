Who We Are

Built on Expertise. Driven by Trust.

Biztech Group Pte Ltd was founded in 2015 with a singular vision: to provide Singapore businesses with a truly integrated technology partner — one who could handle everything from physical security to IT infrastructure under one roof.

Over 11 years, we have grown from a small security installation team into a full-service technology solutions company. Today, our 60+ strong team of engineers, project managers, and support specialists serves clients across every major industry in Singapore — from MNCs and government bodies to SMEs, schools, and condominiums.

What sets us apart is our commitment to long-term partnerships. We don't just install and leave. We stay with our clients through maintenance, upgrades, and expansion — becoming a trusted extension of their operations team.

Our Journey

11 Years of Milestones

2015

Founded in Singapore

Biztech Group was established with a focus on delivering CCTV and access control solutions to SMEs, laying the foundation for long-term growth built on trust and technical excellence.

2017

Expanded into Network Infrastructure

Expanded capabilities to include structured cabling and network infrastructure solutions. Secured our first enterprise client and began supporting larger commercial deployments.

2019

ISO 9001 & GeBIZ Registration

Achieved ISO 9001 certification and GeBIZ supplier registration, opening opportunities to participate in government and public sector projects.

2022

Smart Building Division Launched

Launched our Smart Building and IoT division to support the growing demand for integrated technologies. The team expanded to more than 40 specialists across multiple disciplines.

2026

3,500+ Projects Milestone

Surpassed 3,500 regional deployments and grew into a team of more than 60 professionals, supporting over 200 active clients across diverse industries throughout the region.
