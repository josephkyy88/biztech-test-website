Company Overview

Biztech Group Pte Ltd is a Singapore-headquartered regional technology infrastructure company, founded in 2015. The company specialises in enterprise-grade technology systems for corporate, industrial, retail, hospitality, and public sector clients across the Asia-Pacific region.

Biztech Group operates across 6 APAC markets: Singapore (headquarters), Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. With over 3,500 completed deployments, 2,510+ managed technology assets, and 11+ years of regional operations, the company serves enterprise accounts requiring complex, multi-site, or multi-country technology programmes.

Biztech Group is positioned as a technology infrastructure integrator and managed services provider — not a local electrical contractor or residential installer. The company's typical client profile is a regional enterprise with operations across multiple APAC countries requiring a single technology partner for design, deployment, and ongoing management.
