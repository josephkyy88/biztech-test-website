1Introduction & Scope

Biztech Group Pte Ltd ("Biztech Group", "we", "our", or "us"), a company incorporated in Singapore (UEN: 201728418N), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal data you share with us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and protect personal data in connection with:

  • use of our website at www.biztechgroup.sg and any related subdomains;
  • submission of requests for quotation (RFQ), enquiry forms, or contact requests;
  • engagement with our AI-assisted budgetary estimation tools;
  • any business relationship, correspondence, or service agreement you enter into with us.

This Policy applies to individuals whose personal data is processed by Biztech Group, including website visitors, prospective clients, current clients, vendors, and business contacts. It does not apply to our employees, whose data is governed by separate internal policies.

By using our website or submitting any form on this site, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

7Cross-Border Data Transfers

Biztech Group operates across the Asia Pacific region. In delivering our services, your personal data may be transferred to, stored in, or processed in countries outside Singapore, including Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, the Philippines, and Australia.

Any cross-border transfer of personal data is conducted in accordance with Section 26 of the PDPA and the PDPC's transfer limitation obligation. We ensure that equivalent standards of data protection apply to transferred data by:

  • entering into binding data transfer agreements with overseas recipients that impose obligations no less onerous than those under the PDPA;
  • transferring only the minimum personal data necessary for the purpose of the transfer;
  • ensuring that overseas recipients have in place technical and organisational measures to protect transferred data.

10AI-Assisted Budgetary Estimates

Important Notice: Our website may incorporate AI-assisted tools that generate indicative budgetary estimates based on inputs you provide (such as property type, floor area, or number of cameras). These estimates are generated by automated systems and are for preliminary planning purposes only. They do not constitute a formal quotation, offer, or contractual commitment.

When you use an AI-assisted estimation tool on our website:

  • The inputs you provide (e.g., property description, scope parameters) may be processed by our AI systems and third-party AI service providers to generate an estimate;
  • We do not link AI tool inputs to your personal identity unless you voluntarily provide contact information in the same session;
  • Inputs provided to AI tools may be used in aggregated, anonymised form to improve the accuracy and quality of our estimation models;
  • No automated decision with legal or similarly significant effect is made solely on the basis of AI tool outputs without human review.

All formal quotations are prepared by our certified consultants following an on-site survey and supersede any AI-generated estimate. See our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer on AI estimates.

11Your Rights Under the PDPA

As a data subject under the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (Singapore), you have the following rights with respect to your personal data held by Biztech Group:

  • Right of Access — You may request confirmation of whether we hold personal data about you, and a copy of that data. We will respond within 30 days of receiving a valid request.
  • Right of Correction — If personal data we hold about you is inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading, you may request that we correct it. We will effect corrections within a reasonable time.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where our processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to Data Portability — Where technically feasible, you may request that we transmit your personal data to another organisation in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Cease Unauthorised Use — If we are processing your personal data without a valid legal basis, you may request that we stop such processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a written request to our Data Protection Officer at dpo@biztechgroup.sg. We may require proof of identity before processing your request. We reserve the right to charge a reasonable administrative fee for access requests as permitted under the PDPA.

If you are dissatisfied with our handling of your personal data or your request, you may lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) of Singapore at www.pdpc.gov.sg.

12Data Security

Biztech Group implements reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal, or similar risks. These measures include, but are not limited to:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS protocols;
  • Access controls limiting personal data access to authorised personnel only;
  • Regular security assessments and staff training on data protection;
  • Secure disposal procedures for physical and electronic records;
  • Incident response procedures in the event of a data breach.

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in significant harm to affected individuals, we will notify the PDPC and affected individuals in accordance with the PDPA's mandatory breach notification obligations (within 3 calendar days of assessment, where required).

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your personal data.
