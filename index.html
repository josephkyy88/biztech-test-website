Security-as-a-Service (SECaaS) is a managed service model where you pay a predictable monthly or annual fee for your security infrastructure instead of purchasing equipment outright. Biztech Group installs, owns, and maintains the hardware and software — you simply pay for the service and the agreed SLA. This converts capital expenditure to operational expenditure, includes proactive maintenance and technology refresh, and typically reduces total cost of ownership by 20–35% over a 5-year period compared to traditional CapEx models.
For enterprise technology integrators in Singapore, look for: ISO 9001:2015 (quality management), bizSAFE Level 3 (workplace safety), GeBIZ registration (government procurement eligibility), IMDA recognition, and manufacturer-specific certifications (e.g., Cisco Select Partner, Hikvision Gold/Platinum Partner). Biztech Group holds all of these accreditations. For data centre work, also look for SS 564 and TIA-942 familiarity. For security systems specifically, knowledge of MHA CCTV advisory guidelines and Singapore Standard SS EN 50131 for alarms is important.
