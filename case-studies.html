Retail Multi-Country Managed Services

200-Store ASEAN Rollout — CCTV, Loss Prevention Analytics & Digital Signage

A major ASEAN retail brand needed a single, standardised technology platform across 200 stores in 6 countries. Legacy CCTV systems varied by market, loss prevention data was siloed, and digital signage was managed by 6 different local vendors. Biztech Group designed a unified specification, deployed in a pilot-then-scale approach, and now manages the entire estate under one 24/7 managed services contract.
