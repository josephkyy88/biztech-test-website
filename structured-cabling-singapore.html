Talk to a Cabling Specialist

Structured cabling is a standardised approach to building network infrastructure — organising cables, patch panels, and distribution points into a documented, vendor-neutral system. Every enterprise technology (IP cameras, VoIP, Wi-Fi, BMS) runs over it. Poor cabling is the most common cause of unexplained network issues and technology underperformance.

Cat6 supports 1Gbps up to 55m — suitable for most offices. Cat6A supports 10Gbps up to 100m — recommended for data centres and PoE++ environments. Fibre optic supports 40Gbps+ over long distances and is immune to interference — standard for building backbones and inter-building connections. We recommend the appropriate grade based on application requirements and future bandwidth needs.

We design to TIA-568 (ANSI), ISO/IEC 11801, and SS 388 (Singapore Standard). All installations are documented with as-built drawings, cable schedules, and test reports using Fluke DSX testers. Channel certification reports are provided for every data point upon project completion.
